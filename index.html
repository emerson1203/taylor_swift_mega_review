Taylor Swift: A review of all of her songs

Why did I do this?

I spent a... long amount of time carefully listening to and reading the lyrics of every Taylor Swift song to provide my personal thoughts and feelings on all of them. For fun. Because of Midnights. Idk why. I just did.

Now, for some rules and regulations. Songs are rated out of 10, then all song ratings are averaged to find the album rating out of ten. If there is a Taylor's Version, that was reviewed. Otherwise if there was a Deluxe version that was reviewed. Otherwise, simply the normal album was reviewed.
